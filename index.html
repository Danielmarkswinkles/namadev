our story


Our passion, philosophy and experience defines who we are. At Nama, we take an intelligent approach to development, by curating unique residential projects that respond to their surrounds and are designed to cater for a contemporary lifestyle.

We place a large emphasis on strong architectural outcomes that focus on liveability, spatial experience, and quality materials to deliver small to medium residential developments that add value to the community. With 35 years of combined experience in architecture, interiors, construction and real estate, Nama is committed to delivering homes with heart and soul, and to providing long lasting value to its inhabitants.
